Privacy Policy

Last updated: Aril 16, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Tic Tac Toe: Classic Board Game ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not require account registration or login.

The following data is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted:

  • Game scores (wins, draws, losses)
  • Selected theme preference
  • Sound on/off preference
  • AI difficulty preference

3. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your consent choices. This includes:

  • Advertising ID (resettable device identifier)
  • IP address (used for approximate location to serve regional ads)
  • App interaction data

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom, we display a consent form before showing any ads, in compliance with GDPR. You may withdraw or change your consent at any time using the "Ad Privacy Settings" option in the app.

For more information on how Google uses advertising data, visit: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Retention

All locally stored preferences and scores remain on your device until you uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data.

6. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
